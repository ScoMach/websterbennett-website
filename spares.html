Machine Identification

How to Find Your Serial Number

The serial number plate is located on the left-hand side of the cross rail, recessed above the lower way surface.

It's a small metal tag stamped or engraved with the machine serial number. Look along the vertical face of the cross rail casting, just above where the rail sits on the column ways.

Tip: Also note the table diameter (stamped on the table face or listed on a second plate) — this helps us identify the exact parts for your configuration.
