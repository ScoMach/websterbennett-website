Table Drive Shear Pin

Part Number: WB599

🇺🇸 In Stock USA — Ships Immediately

The shear pin is a deliberate weak link in the table drive train — it is designed to shear before the gearbox or clutch is damaged by overload. A sheared pin is the most common cause of a stalled table on any W&B machine. This is a consumable — shops running heavy cuts should keep spares on hand.
