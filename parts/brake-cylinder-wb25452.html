Brake Cylinder

Part Number: WB25452

🇬🇧 Ships from W&B UK — Typically 2–4 Weeks

The brake cylinder is the hydraulic actuator for the W&B rotary table brake system. It applies clamping force to the brake shoes to hold the table stationary when the drive is disengaged. A failing brake cylinder is a safety issue — the table can rotate under cutting load if the brake cannot hold.

Compatibility

  • All DH, M, EM, N, EV, and R series machines
  • Confirm exact variant with serial number — early DH may differ from later production

Order History

8 orders on file — second most frequently ordered part

Paired With

Often ordered together with KB1124 (brake shoes). If the cylinder is leaking, inspect the shoes for glazing or wear at the same time.

Technical Note: Second most ordered part in our history (8 orders). If a customer reports the table drifting under load or slow to stop, check the brake cylinder and shoes first.
