No.5 Ferodo Clutch Disc

Part Number: 8211-109

🇺🇸 In Stock USA — Ships Immediately

The No.5 Ferodo friction disc is the most frequently ordered Webster & Bennett part. It is a critical wear component in the main spindle clutch pack, responsible for transmitting drive torque to the table. Worn Ferodo discs cause clutch slipping, inconsistent table rotation, and eventual loss of drive.

Compatibility

  • Early DH (S/N 5022–5232) — 36" and 48" standard tables (No.5 clutch)
  • Series II DH (S/N 5233–5441) — 36" and 48" tables (No.5 clutch)
  • Series M / EM (S/N 5442–6561) — 36" and 48" standard tables (No.5 clutch)

Order History

9 orders on file — our most frequently ordered part

Paired With

Always ordered in sets with the matching steel drive disc. For Early DH: pair with 8211-112 (thin). For Series II DH: pair with 8211-110 (thick/splined). For M/EM: pair with 8211-111 (spur-drive).

Technical Note: The Ferodo disc is the same across all three series (8211-109). The steel disc differs by series — confirming the series is essential before ordering a set.
