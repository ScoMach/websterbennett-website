Main Thrust Bearing

Part Number: 8400-129

🇬🇧 Ships from W&B UK — Typically 2–4 Weeks

The main thrust bearing supports the axial load of the spindle and headstock assembly. It is critical for maintaining spindle accuracy and preventing axial play, which directly affects part quality in boring and turning operations. Worn thrust bearings cause rumble, vibration, and dimensional inaccuracy.
