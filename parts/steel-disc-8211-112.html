Clutch & Brake · 8211-112

No.5 Steel Clutch Disc — Early DH

Part Number: 8211-112

🇬🇧 Ships from W&B UK — Typically 2–4 Weeks

The thin steel drive disc for No.5 clutch assemblies on Early DH machines. This disc alternates with the Ferodo friction disc (8211-109) in the clutch pack. The Early DH version is thinner than later variants — it is not interchangeable with the Series II DH thick disc (8211-110) or the M/EM spur-drive disc (8211-111).

Compatibility

  • Early DH (S/N 5022–5232) — 36" and 48" standard tables (No.5 clutch only)

Order History

6 orders on file

Paired With

Always ordered with 8211-109 (Ferodo disc). A complete clutch rebuild requires matched sets of steel and Ferodo discs plus 6 spring/plunger assemblies (8473-09).

Technical Note: The steel disc part number is the key identifier for the series: 8211-112 = Early DH thin, 8211-110 = Series II DH thick/splined, 8211-111 = M/EM spur-drive. Getting this wrong means the clutch will not assemble or engage correctly.
