Saddle Nut

Part Number: WB730

🇺🇸 In Stock USA — Ships Immediately

The saddle nut threads onto the cross-slide leadscrew and converts rotary motion from the feed box into linear travel of the tool saddle. As it wears, backlash develops — the saddle drifts and positioning accuracy degrades. Saddle nut replacement restores precision without a full saddle rebuild.

Compatibility

  • Multiple W&B series — confirm exact variant by serial number
  • Variants: WB730, WB731, WB921, WB922 — different machines use different nuts
