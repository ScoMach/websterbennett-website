No.6 Ferodo Clutch Disc

Part Number: 8211-113

🇬🇧 Ships from W&B UK — Typically 2–4 Weeks

The No.6 Ferodo friction disc for larger clutch assemblies. Used on machines with 60" or 72" tables across all series, 48" S.H.S. (Special High Speed) on Early DH, 48" High on M/EM, and all table sizes on Series V/EV/R machines (which use No.6 clutch regardless of table diameter).

Compatibility

  • Early DH (S/N 5022–5232) — 48" S.H.S. and 60" tables
  • Series II DH (S/N 5233–5441) — 60" tables
  • Series M / EM (S/N 5442–6561) — 48" High, 60", 72" tables
  • Series V / EV / R (S/N 6562+) — ALL table sizes (always No.6)
